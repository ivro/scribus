Community Resources

The Scribus mailing list is on-line at http://nashi.altmuehlnet.de/mailman/listinfo/scribus. There is both an English and German language interface to the mailing list manager. This is for both end-user support and development issues.

Mailing List Etiquette

The Scribus mailing list is active and very friendly. Do not be suprised if your questions receive several replies within a day. That said, there are a few informal rules we (and most list readers) like folks to follow:

  • Don't Send Html email to the list. Why? Many list subscribers use text based e-mail readers and they see raw html. More on sending HTML email: Why HTML E-mail is bad.
  • Avoid attachments. It is better to upload files to a web space, then provide a link in your e-mail.
  • Avoid Top Posting: Here is why. Many list readers get the digest format which really loses readability with top posting.

The mailing list may be searched at this address: http://search.srem.org/

The Bug Tracker is located at http://bugs.scribus.net/

The Wiki is located at http://wiki.scribus.net/

An IRC channel #scribus on freenode.net is quite active. There is usually at least one person from the core development team present there. For immediate assistance, this is the best along with the mail list, but does not excuse not searching the docs :-) See: Scribus IRC

The Scribus Developers maintain a blog at: http://rants.scribus.net/. This would be of interest to anyone using Scribus.
